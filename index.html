Who Am I

Senior iOS Developer with 9+ years delivering mobile products across fintech, banking, and consumer apps. I specialise in software architecture, modular design, and security building systems that minimise technical debt while keeping team velocity high.

Most of my recent work is in large-scale banking infrastructure, at Softtech I owned the ORMComponents / ORMFramework SDK powering İşCep, used by 14M+ users, and adopted by 10+ internal teams. On the security side I maintain PinGuard, an open-source iOS SDK for SSL pinning and mTLS with zero runtime dependencies.

I care about clean abstractions, SOLID principles, and reviews that teach rather than gate. I've built iOS features from scratch, led legacy-to-modern migrations (UIKit → SwiftUI + MVVM), defined modularisation standards, and introduced CI/CD quality gates that stick.

Open to senior iOS roles and select consulting engagements in fintech, banking, or security adjacent products. Istanbul based, open to remote.
